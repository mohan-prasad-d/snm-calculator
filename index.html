About This Suite
What is this?

A premium numerical methods calculator suite organized into three comprehensive units covering algebraic equations, matrix methods, numerical integration, interpolation, and differential equations. Each tool goes beyond simple answers — it shows every formula substituted with your actual input values, step-by-step, so you can follow along, learn, and verify your hand calculations.

Designed for engineering and mathematics students tackling complex problems in linear algebra, calculus, and differential equations. Built using math.js for safe expression parsing, Chart.js for solution visualization, and pure HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no install.
